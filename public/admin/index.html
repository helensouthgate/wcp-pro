Sync schedule & results

Fixtures and results sync automatically every 15 minutes from football-data.org. Use this to pull the latest data immediately (e.g. right after deploy).

AI predictions

Predictions refresh automatically every day at 6am UK time for upcoming matches in the next 14 days — score, confidence, reasoning, and who in the office wins or loses. Use this to regenerate that window now.

Manual score entry

Elimination overrides

Teams are eliminated automatically from results. Use these only to correct an edge case. Click a tag to clear the override.
